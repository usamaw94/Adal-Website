Advocate Arslan Chaudhry completed his LL.B along with a Diploma in Corporate Law from Punjab University in 2011. He went on to do his Masters of Law and Business from Bucerius Law School, Germany after which he trained as a Corporate Lawyer at Herfurth & Partner, Hannover, Germany. His primary areas of practice included Corporate and Commercial Law.

He returned to Pakistan in 2012 and joined as an associate at the Legal 500 firm HaidermotaBNR & Co where he build upon his knowledge and experience of litigation and advisory in Corporate and Commercial Law, and successfully represented several multinational companies in litigation based as well as arbitration disputes. Additionally, during his time at HaidermotaBNR Advocate Arslan Chaudhry gained sufficient experience of representing and advising clients on matters pertaining to company, contract, constitutional, property, customs and excise, intellectual property and international law.

Advocate Arslan Chaudhry has also assisted multinational clients in the Competition Commission of Pakistan and has a wealth of experience in mergers and acquisitions of companies. Advocate Arslan Chaudhry is a compassionate individual who has always considered a client’s problem as his own and seen it through till the very end.

Advocate Arslan Chaudhry is a well-renowned advocate of the High Courts of Pakistan and is well versed with the procedures of, and executing procedural requirements such as planning, registration and setting up of new businesses, protecting their intellectual property rights and safeguarding their commercial transactions.
